## Stay in the loop

The best way to follow progress is to **subscribe to the RSS feed** — every new post will show up there the moment it's published.


Drop that URL into your feed reader of choice ([Miniflux](https://miniflux.app), [NetNewsWire](https://netnewswire.com), [Feedly](https://feedly.com), or any RSS client) and you'll get notified as soon as there's something new.
